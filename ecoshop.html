EcoShop

Product system to encourage consumers to make environmentally conscious purchases.

Capstone Project

10 weeks

overview


For my fall senior capstone, I wanted to try to tackle an overarching world problem with a digital solution. I wanted to focus on how the food industry contributes to climate change, and how product and experience design could alter consumer behavior to lower individual environmental impacts.

EcoShop consists of two parts: an in store checkout system that shows a consumer their "Eco Impact Score," and a mobile app that can be downloaded at the checkout or outside of the store. Receipts can be sent to the app, or scanned in afterwards. The app features a receipt scanner, food barcode scanner to display the environmental information of a product, and a grocery list maker.

The ultimate goal of EcoShop is to make green grocery shopping more approachable and easier. Using EcoShop, users can track their past purchases, learn more about how their foods contribute to their environmental impact and ways they could lower their impact.

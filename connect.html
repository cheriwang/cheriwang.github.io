Connect

App concept to encourage users to help support local businesses in the community impacted by COVID-19.

Personal Project

Apr 2020

overview


In Studio, we were prompted to design something relevant to COVID-19 and how we could help. After seeing so many of my favorite Minneapolis cafes and coffee shops close down (goodbye Sleepy V’s & Anelace 🙁 I’ll miss you), I knew I wanted to do something that focused on encouraging people to support local businesses and restaurants.

With the economic impacts due to COVID-19, many small businesses are facing permanent closure, and by supporting your favorite local spots, you can help your community through times of hardship with Connect.

Although this was a short project, I really wanted to focus on the visual design and the micro-interactions to help the prototype feel more dynamic rather than still wireframes.

Interaction Design


Collection of some personal and class projects focusing on interface design and interactive experience.

outbound


This was a UI focused project I did in Interaction Design in the Fall of 2020. We were prompted to create an app concept of our choice, so I came up with Outbound, a camping trip planner to help users plan a roadtrip route, book campsites, and choose hikes.



This project was designed and prototyped in Figma, and you can view the clickable prototype here.

connect


In this Product Design Studio project from Spring 2020, we were prompted to design something relevant to COVID-19 and how we could help. After seeing so many of my favorite Minneapolis cafes and coffee shops close down, I knew I wanted to do something that focused on encouraging people to support local businesses and restaurants.



This project was designed and prototyped in Adobe XD. You can view the clickable prototype here.

space clock


For this project from Interaction Design, we were prompted to create a design in Adobe After Effects that uses time to effectively frame a message or information. I love space and wanted to create a wallpaper-like display (think Momentum, the Google Chrome extension), so I created my design around that.

In my design, for every minute, the earth illustration revolves once around the sun. Additionally, I wanted to play with time dilation in space and on different planets, so periodically the time will shift to how it would move on a different planet.



circle: prescription reminder system


In this Product Design Studio project from Fall 2020, we were prompted to design a wearable tracker and associated app interface. Medical non-adherence is a really prevalent problem, so I wanted to design a product system for those recently entering adulthood to take better control of their health.

Circle consists of an electric pill organizer that logs when you've taken your prescription from the opening and closing of the case, a reminder ring, as well as an associated app.





The interface for this project was designed and prototyped in Adobe XD.

Apple Activity Desktop


This was a quick personal project I did in the Summer of 2019. Apple currently does not have a desktop version of their activity app, so I wanted to explore extending this product system and utilizing their UI and brand guidelines to create an desktop activity app. I browsed around the existing Apple desktop apps, like the App Store, Notes, and Calendar apps to pull inspiration for this project.

Apple has since updated their activity iPhone app so there are some missing aspects in this prototype, but it was still a fun project that I will revisit!

This project was created in Adobe XD. You can view the prototype here

Aquarech


Aquarech is an app to help Kenyan fish farmers organize their ponds and cages, to better manage and sell their fish stock. In the Summer of 2019, I worked with Kate and Dave, the co-founders of Aquarech to design an interface to be developed.

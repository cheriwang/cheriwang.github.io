space clock


Project Concept
For this project, we were prompted to create a design in Adobe After Effects that uses time to effectively frame a message or information. I love space and wanted to create a wallpaper-like display (think Momentum, the Google Chrome extension), so I created my design around that.

In my design, for every minute, the earth illustration revolves once around the sun. Additionally, I wanted to play with time dilation in space and on different planets, so periodically the time will shift to how it would move on a different planet.

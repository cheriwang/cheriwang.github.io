Hello! I'm Cheri 👋

Nice to meet you!

I'm a recent graduate of the University of Minnesota with a B.S in Product Design and User Experience. While I'm a Minnesotan at heart, I'm currently living in Chicago where I design with Publicis Sapient.

My freshman year of college, I switched my major four (yes, four!) times before I finally found product design. It felt like the perfect mix of problem solving, solution building, and most importantly, creativity- something that every other major I had considered was missing. I love being able to exercise both sides of my brain in the work that I do, and the opportunity to improve the lives of others through design drives me.

Aside from design, I'm passionate about sustainability and wellness, and I love the outdoors, live music, and houseplants. In my free time, you can find me on a run by the lakefront, at the bouldering gym, or enjoying an oat milk cortado with an everything bagel!

Thanks for stopping by 😁

a little about me

I am a senior at the University of Minnesota graduating this May with a B.S in Product Design and User Experience, and a minor in Information Technology.

As pre-med turned design student, I am so grateful to have found a discipline that allows me to think analytically and creatively every day. The intersection of technology and humanity absolutely fascinates me, and I strive to design products that will benefit users and create an impact.

I am always eager to learn more, and am looking to enter the design consulting space after graduation.

Aside from school, I am an avid concert goer, board game lover, and outdoors enthusiast. In my free time, you can find me on a walk or run around Minneapolis, or on the lookout for the next best cup of coffee in the cities! A fun fact about me is that I (clearly) love using emojis! 😃👍

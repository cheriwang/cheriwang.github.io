Hello! I'm Cheri Wang 👋

Nice to meet you!

I'm a recent graduate of the University of Minnesota (go gophers!) with a B.S in Product Design and User Experience. Although I'm a born and raised Minnesotan, I'm currently based in Chicago where I work as an Experience Designer at Publicis Sapient.

As a former pre-med student, I've always wanted to make some sort of impact on the world with my career. Over time, I've found that the intersection of technology and humans absolutely fascinates me, and that I want to make an impact using design.

I love spending time outdoors, the NYT daily crossword, and house plants. In my free time, you can find me on a run around the city, or on the lookout for the next best iced Americano. A fun fact about me is that I (clearly) love using emojis! 😃👍
